Enum TextureRect.StretchMode

Namespace: Robust.Client.UserInterface.Controls
Assembly: Robust.Client.dll
Syntax
public enum StretchMode : byte

Fields

Name Description
Keep

The texture is drawn in its correct size, in the top left corner of the control.
KeepAspect

The texture is stretched to take as much space as possible, while maintaining the original aspect ratio. The texture is positioned from the top left corner of the control. The texture remains completely visible, potentially leaving some sections of the control blank.
KeepAspectCentered

KeepAspect, but the texture is centered instead.
KeepAspectCovered

KeepAspectCentered, but the texture covers the entire control, potentially cutting out part of the texture.
KeepCentered

The texture is drawn in its correct size, in the center of the control.
Scale

The texture is stretched to fit the entire area of the control.
Tile

The texture is tiled to fit the entire area of the control, without stretching.
